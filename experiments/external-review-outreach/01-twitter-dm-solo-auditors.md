## Personalisation tips

- Read their pinned tweet first; mention a recent thing they reviewed
  that's tangentially relevant. Generic DMs get ignored.
- Keep the first message under the tweet limit so they can reply in
  one tweet if interested.
- Don't follow up more than once per week.

## Order to post

1. SecureumDAO first — most likely to get a response from a Wizard
2. Cyfrin second — community-friendly, Patrick may RT
3. Code4rena third — wardens might cross-post
4. Smaller / niche channels fourth

## What NOT to do

- Don't cross-post the exact same message in 10 servers simultaneously.
  Curate per-channel — Discord moderators flag spam.
- Don't @-mention specific people in a public channel; DM them instead.
- Don't post the same message twice in the same channel within 48 hours.
